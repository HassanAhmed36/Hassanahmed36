Guinness Press System

The Guinness Press System is an article publishing platform designed to receive submissions from authors. The website is managed via a control dashboard and features three roles: author, board member, and admin. All modules are described below:

  • Submission Management: Authors can submit articles after registering themselves. The submission process involves a multi-step workflow. Initially, the author submits the article and pays the Article Processing Charge (APC). Once payment is received, the submission is assigned to board members for review.
  • Peer Review Management: After payment, the article is assigned to a board member for peer review. The board member reviews the article and either approves or rejects it.
  • Publication Management: Once an article is approved, it moves to the publication stage. Articles are organized into issues, which belong to volumes connected to journals. The management of journals, volumes, issues, and articles is done based on their publishing date.
  • Payment and Notifications: The platform integrates the PayPal API for APC payments and automated email notifications to keep authors informed at every submission stage.
  • Technologies and Metadata: The application is built using Laravel, Bootstrap, jQuery, MySQL, and XML. It generates XML metadata for seamless submission to external platforms.
